📜 Community Rules

Guidelines to keep PixelLumo safe, creative, and fun for all.

Community Guidelines

  • Be kind and respectful to everyone.
  • No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination.
  • Share only your own creations or with proper credit.
  • No NSFW or illegal content.
  • Respect privacy—no doxxing or sharing private info.
  • Follow staff instructions and moderation decisions.

Moderation Policy

  • Warnings, mutes, or bans for rule violations.
  • Appeals can be made via Support.
  • Staff decisions are final to keep the community safe.
